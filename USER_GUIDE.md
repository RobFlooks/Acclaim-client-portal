## Welcome to Acclaim Portal

Welcome to the Acclaim Credit Management & Recovery Portal. This secure online platform has been designed to give you complete control and visibility over your debt recovery cases, enabling you to:

- **Track case progress** in real-time
- **Communicate securely** with our recovery team
- **View and download** case documents
- **Monitor payments** and recovery performance
- **Generate comprehensive reports** for your records
- **Submit new cases** directly through the portal

This guide will walk you through all the features available to you and help you get the most out of the portal.

---

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal

1. **Open your web browser** and navigate to your portal URL (provided by Acclaim)
2. **You will be directed to the login page**

### First-Time Login

When you first receive your account credentials:

1. **Enter your username** (typically your email address)
2. **Enter the temporary password** provided by the administrator
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. **You will be prompted to change your password immediately** for security purposes
5. Create a **strong, unique password** following these requirements:
   - At least 8 characters long
   - Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
   - Include numbers and special characters
6. **Confirm your new password** and click "Change Password"
7. You're now ready to use the portal!

### Security Tips

- **Never share your password** with anyone
- **Log out when finished**, especially on shared computers
- **Change your password regularly** for enhanced security
- Your session will automatically expire after a period of inactivity

## Dashboard Overview

When you log in, you'll land on your **Dashboard** - your central hub for all case activity.

### Dashboard Features

#### Quick Statistics
At the top of your dashboard, you'll see key metrics:

- **Active Cases**: Number of cases currently being worked on
- **Closed Cases**: Successfully resolved cases
- **Total Debt**: Combined value of all outstanding debt across your cases
- **Total Recovered**: Amount successfully recovered to date

#### Recent Activity
View the latest updates on your cases, including:
- New messages from the recovery team
- Document uploads
- Payment updates
- Status changes

#### Navigation Menu

The sidebar (or top menu on mobile) provides quick access to:

- **Dashboard**: Overview and statistics (home)
- **Cases**: View and manage all your cases
- **Messages**: Communication centre
- **Documents**: View and download case documents
- **Reports**: Generate detailed reports
- **Profile**: Manage your account settings

## Managing Your Cases

The **Cases** section is where you'll spend most of your time, monitoring and managing your debt recovery cases.

### Viewing Your Cases

1. Click **"Cases"** in the navigation menu
2. You'll see a **list of all your cases**, showing:
   - Case reference number
   - Debtor name
   - Outstanding amount
   - Current status
   - Recovery stage
   - Last update date

### Case Filters & Search

**Search Bar**: Type debtor name, case reference, or account number to quickly find specific cases

**Filter Options**:
- **By Status**: Active, In Progress, On Hold, Closed
- **By Stage**: Initial Contact, Legal Action, Payment Plan, etc.
- **By Debtor Type**: Individual or Organisation
- **By Amount**: Filter by debt value ranges

**Sort Options**: Click column headers to sort by:
- Case reference
- Debtor name
- Amount (ascending/descending)
- Date created
- Last updated

### Viewing Case Details

Click on any case to view full details:

#### Case Information Tab
- Debtor details (name, address, contact information)
- Debt information (original amount, interest, fees, costs)
- Current outstanding balance
- Case status and stage
- Date created and last updated
- Assigned case handler

#### Activity History Tab
View a complete timeline of all actions taken on the case:
- Initial case creation
- Contact attempts
- Legal proceedings
- Status changes
- Payment updates
- Notes from the recovery team

#### Messages Tab
- View all messages related to this specific case
- Send new messages to the recovery team about this case
- Attach files to your messages (up to 10MB)

#### Documents Tab
- View all documents associated with the case
- Download invoices, agreements, and legal documents
- Upload supporting documents

#### Payments Tab
View complete payment history:
- Payment date and amount
- Payment method
- Reference number
- Running balance

## Messages & Communication

The **Messages** section is your direct communication channel with the Acclaim recovery team.

### Viewing Messages

1. Click **"Messages"** in the navigation menu
2. You'll see:
   - **Inbox**: All received messages
   - **Sent**: Messages you've sent
   - **Unread badge**: Number of unread messages

### Sending a Message

#### General Message (Not Case-Specific)
1. Click **"Compose New Message"**
2. Enter a **Subject line**
3. Type your **message content**
4. Optionally **attach files** (up to 10MB)
5. Click **"Send"**

#### Case-Specific Message
1. Go to the **Cases** section
2. Open the relevant case
3. Click the **"Messages"** tab
4. Click **"Send Message"**
5. Enter subject and message
6. Attach files if needed
7. Click **"Send"**

**Important**: Case-specific messages automatically include case details in the notification to our team, ensuring faster response times.

### Message Features

- **Email Notifications**: You'll receive email notifications for new messages (configurable in Profile settings)
- **File Attachments**: Attach relevant documents (invoices, proof of payment, correspondence)
- **Read Receipts**: See when messages have been read
- **Message History**: Complete conversation history is preserved

### Response Times

- **Standard queries**: Within 2 business days
- **Urgent matters**: Within 4 business hours (mark as "Urgent" in subject)
- **Payment notifications**: Acknowledged within 1 business day

## Document Management

Access all case-related documents in one secure location.

### Viewing Documents

1. Click **"Documents"** in the navigation menu
2. You'll see all documents across your cases, or:
3. View case-specific documents in the **Documents tab** within each case

### Document Information

For each document, you can view:
- Document name
- Document type (Invoice, Agreement, Legal Notice, etc.)
- Upload date
- File size
- Associated case reference

### Downloading Documents

1. Find the document you need
2. Click the **"Download"** icon or button
3. The document will download to your computer
4. Documents are typically in PDF format

### Uploading Documents

To upload documents to a specific case:

1. Navigate to the case
2. Go to the **Documents** tab
3. Click **"Upload Document"**
4. Select the file from your computer (max 10MB)
5. Add a description (optional)
6. Click **"Upload"**

**Supported file types**: PDF, Word documents (.doc, .docx), Excel spreadsheets (.xls, .xlsx), images (.jpg, .png), and other common formats.

## Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports to analyse case performance and track recovery progress.

### Available Reports

Access reports by clicking **"Reports"** in the navigation menu.

#### 1. Case Summary Report

**What it shows**:
- Complete list of all your cases
- Key details for each case (debtor, amounts, status)
- Summary statistics

**Best for**: Getting a complete overview of your portfolio

**Export options**: Excel, PDF

#### 2. Recovery Analysis Report

**What it shows**:
- Original debt vs. amount recovered
- Recovery rates by case
- Outstanding balances
- Performance trends

**Best for**: Analysing recovery effectiveness

**Export options**: Excel, PDF

#### 3. Payment Performance Report

**What it shows**:
- Payment history across all cases
- Payment trends over time
- Average payment values

**Best for**: Tracking payment patterns and cash flow

**Export options**: Excel, PDF

#### 4. Monthly Statement Report

**What it shows**:
- All activity for a specific month

1. Navigate to **Reports**

### Report Tips

- **Save regular reports** for your records
- **Schedule monthly exports** for accounting purposes
- **Compare reports month-over-month** to track trends
- **Use filters** to focus on specific subsets of cases

## Submitting New Cases

Submit new debt recovery cases directly through the portal for faster processing.

### How to Submit a New Case

1. Click your **profile icon** or navigate to the menu
2. Select **"Submit New Case"**
3. You'll see a comprehensive submission form

### Required Information

Complete all sections of the form:

#### 1. Case Details
- Case name/description
- Your internal reference number (optional)

#### 2. Client Information
- Your company name
- Contact person
- Email address
- Phone number

#### 3. Creditor Details (if different from client)
- Creditor name
- Contact information
- Relationship to client

#### 4. Debtor Information
- Debtor type (Individual or Organisation)
- Full name or company name
- Trading name (if applicable)
- Company registration number (for businesses)

#### 5. Debtor Address
- Street address
- Town/City
- County
- Postcode
- Country

#### 6. Debtor Contact Details
- Email address
- Primary phone number
- Secondary phone number (optional)

#### 7. Debt Details
- Original debt amount
- Interest added (if any)
- Costs added (if any)
- Total debt amount
- Brief description of debt

#### 8. Payment Terms (Original Agreement)
- Payment terms
- Due date
- Payment method

#### 9. Invoice Information
- Invoice number(s)
- Invoice date(s)
- Purchase order number (if applicable)

#### 10. Additional Information
- Any relevant notes
- Special instructions
- Previous recovery attempts

#### 11. Supporting Documents
Upload relevant documents:
- Original invoices
- Contracts or agreements
- Correspondence with debtor
- Proof of delivery
- Other supporting documents

**File limit**: 10MB per file, multiple files accepted

### Submitting the Form

1. **Review all information** carefully
2. Ensure **all required fields** are completed
3. **Upload supporting documents**
4. Click **"Submit Case"**
5. You'll receive a **confirmation message** with the case reference number
6. An **email confirmation** will be sent to you
7. Our team will **review and process** your submission within 1-2 business days

### After Submission

- You'll receive an **email notification** when your case has been processed
- The case will appear in your **Cases** section
- You can track progress through the portal
- Our team may contact you if additional information is needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password if I forget it?**  
A: Contact the Acclaim administrator at email@acclaim.law to request a password reset. For security reasons, passwords cannot be reset through the portal.

**Q: Can I access the portal on my mobile phone?**  
A: Yes! The portal is fully responsive and works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile phones.

**Q: How secure is my data?**  
A: Very secure. The portal uses:
- Industry-standard SSL encryption
- Secure authentication
- Regular security audits
- Automatic session timeouts
- Secure database storage

**Q: Can multiple people from my organisation access the portal?**  
A: Yes. Each person needs their own account. Contact your administrator to set up additional users.

### Cases

**Q: How often is case information updated?**  
A: Case information is updated in real-time as actions are taken by our recovery team.

**Q: What does each case status mean?**
- **Active**: Case is currently being worked on
- **In Progress**: Recovery actions are underway
- **On Hold**: Temporary pause (e.g., awaiting debtor response)
- **Closed**: Case successfully resolved or closed

**Q: Can I close a case myself?**  
A: No. Only administrators can close cases. If you wish to close a case, send a message to the recovery team requesting closure.

**Q: Why can't I see some cases?**  
A: You can only view cases associated with your organisation. If you believe a case is missing, contact support.

### Messages

**Q: How quickly will I receive a response to my messages?**  
A: Standard queries receive responses within 2 business days. Urgent matters are addressed within 4 business hours.

**Q: Can I attach files to messages?**  
A: Yes, you can attach files up to 10MB. Supported formats include PDF, Word, Excel, images, and more.

**Q: Will I be notified when I receive a new message?**  
A: Yes, if you have email notifications enabled in your profile settings.

### Documents

**Q: What file formats are supported for upload?**  
A: PDF, Word (.doc, .docx), Excel (.xls, .xlsx), images (.jpg, .png), and most common document formats.

**Q: Is there a limit on file size?**  
A: Yes, the maximum file size is 10MB per file.

**Q: How long are documents stored?**  
A: Documents are stored securely for the duration of the case and for 7 years after case closure (in line with legal requirements).

### Reports

**Q: Can I schedule automatic reports?**  
A: Currently, reports must be generated manually. Contact support if you need regular automated reports.

**Q: What's the difference between Excel and PDF exports?**  
A: Excel exports allow you to further analyse data in spreadsheet software. PDF exports are best for printing and sharing as fixed documents.

**Q: Can I customise report date ranges?**  
A: Yes, most reports allow you to filter by specific date ranges, case status, and other criteria.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The portal isn't loading. What should I do?**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh your browser
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. If the issue persists, contact support

**Q: I'm having trouble uploading documents. What should I do?**  
A: Ensure:
- Your file is under 10MB
- The file format is supported
- You have a stable internet connection
- Try a different browser if the issue continues

**Q: Why was I logged out automatically?**  
A: For security, sessions automatically expire after a period of inactivity. Simply log back in to continue.
