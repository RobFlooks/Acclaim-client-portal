Getting Started

Accessing the Portal

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to your portal URL (provided by Acclaim)
  2. You will be directed to the login page

First-Time Login

When you first receive your account credentials:

  1. Enter your username (typically your email address)
  2. Enter the temporary password provided by the administrator
  3. Click "Sign In"
  4. You will be prompted to change your password immediately for security purposes
  5. Create a strong, unique password following these requirements:
    • At least 8 characters long
    • Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
    • Include numbers and special characters
  6. Confirm your new password and click "Change Password"
  7. You're now ready to use the portal!
‚ö†Ô∏è Security Tips
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out when finished, especially on shared computers
  • Change your password regularly for enhanced security
  • Your session will automatically expire after a period of inactivity

Dashboard Overview

When you log in, you'll land on your Dashboard - your central hub for all case activity.

Quick Statistics

At the top of your dashboard, you'll see key metrics:

  • Active Cases: Number of cases currently being worked on
  • Closed Cases: Successfully resolved cases
  • Total Debt: Combined value of all outstanding debt across your cases
  • Total Recovered: Amount successfully recovered to date

Navigation Menu

The sidebar (or top menu on mobile) provides quick access to:

  • Dashboard: Overview and statistics (home)
  • Cases: View and manage all your cases
  • Messages: Communication centre
  • Documents: View and download case documents
  • Reports: Generate detailed reports
  • Profile: Manage your account settings

Managing Your Cases

The Cases section is where you'll spend most of your time, monitoring and managing your debt recovery cases.

Viewing Your Cases

  1. Click "Cases" in the navigation menu
  2. You'll see a list of all your cases, showing:
    • Case reference number
    • Debtor name
    • Outstanding amount
    • Current status
    • Recovery stage
    • Last update date

Case Filters & Search

Search Bar: Type debtor name, case reference, or account number to quickly find specific cases

Filter Options:

  • By Status: Active, In Progress, On Hold, Closed
  • By Stage: Initial Contact, Legal Action, Payment Plan, etc.
  • By Debtor Type: Individual or Organisation
  • By Amount: Filter by debt value ranges

Viewing Case Details

Click on any case to view full details with these tabs:

Case Information Tab

  • Debtor details (name, address, contact information)
  • Debt information (original amount, interest, fees, costs)
  • Current outstanding balance
  • Case status and stage
  • Date created and last updated

Activity History Tab

View a complete timeline of all actions taken on the case

Messages Tab

View and send messages related to this specific case

Documents Tab

View, download, and upload documents associated with the case

Payments Tab

View complete payment history with dates, amounts, and running balances

Messages & Communication

The Messages section is your direct communication channel with the Acclaim recovery team.

Sending a Message

General Message (Not Case-Specific)

  1. Click "Compose New Message"
  2. Enter a Subject line
  3. Type your message content
  4. Optionally attach files (up to 10MB)
  5. Click "Send"

Case-Specific Message

  1. Go to the Cases section
  2. Open the relevant case
  3. Click the "Messages" tab
  4. Click "Send Message"
  5. Enter subject and message
  6. Click "Send"
üí° Important: Case-specific messages automatically include case details in the notification to our team, ensuring faster response times.

Document Management

Access all case-related documents in one secure location.

Downloading Documents

  1. Find the document you need
  2. Click the "Download" icon or button
  3. The document will download to your computer

Uploading Documents

To upload documents to a specific case:

  1. Navigate to the case
  2. Go to the Documents tab
  3. Click "Upload Document"
  4. Select the file from your computer (max 10MB)
  5. Click "Upload"

Supported file types: PDF, Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, images, and other common formats.

Generating a Report

  1. Navigate to Reports
  2. Select the report type you want
  3. Choose any filters (date range, case status, etc.)
  4. Click "Generate Report"
  5. Review the report on-screen
  6. Click "Export to Excel" or "Export to PDF" to download

Submitting New Cases

Submit new debt recovery cases directly through the portal for faster processing.

Required Information

Complete all sections of the submission form:

  1. Case Details: Case name and your internal reference
  2. Client Information: Your company details and contact
  3. Creditor Details: If different from client
  4. Debtor Information: Full debtor details and type
  5. Debtor Address: Complete address information
  6. Debtor Contact Details: Email and phone numbers
  7. Debt Details: Original amount, interest, costs, total
  8. Payment Terms: Original agreement terms
  9. Invoice Information: Invoice numbers and dates
  10. Additional Information: Relevant notes and instructions
  11. Supporting Documents: Upload invoices, contracts, correspondence
‚úÖ After Submission
  • You'll receive a confirmation message with the case reference number
  • An email confirmation will be sent to you
  • Our team will review and process your submission within 1-2 business days
  • The case will appear in your Cases section

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How do I reset my password if I forget it?

A: Contact the Acclaim administrator at email@acclaim.law to request a password reset. For security reasons, passwords cannot be reset through the portal.

Q: Can I access the portal on my mobile phone?

A: Yes! The portal is fully responsive and works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile phones.

Q: How secure is my data?

A: Very secure. The portal uses industry-standard SSL encryption, secure authentication, regular security audits, and automatic session timeouts.

Cases

Q: How often is case information updated?

A: Case information is updated in real-time as actions are taken by our recovery team.

Q: What does each case status mean?

  • Active: Case is currently being worked on
  • In Progress: Recovery actions are underway
  • On Hold: Temporary pause (e.g., awaiting debtor response)
  • Closed: Case successfully resolved or closed

Messages

Q: How quickly will I receive a response to my messages?

A: Standard queries receive responses within 2 business days. Urgent matters are addressed within 4 business hours.

Q: Can I attach files to messages?

A: Yes, you can attach files up to 10MB. Supported formats include PDF, Word, Excel, images, and more.

Documents

Q: What file formats are supported for upload?

A: PDF, Word (.doc, .docx), Excel (.xls, .xlsx), images (.jpg, .png), and most common document formats.

Q: Is there a limit on file size?

A: Yes, the maximum file size is 10MB per file.
