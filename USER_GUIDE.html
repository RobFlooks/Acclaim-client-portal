üîß Troubleshooting & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì Navigation & Performance

Q: Why do I only see 20 items per page?

A: The system uses pagination to ensure fast loading times, especially when you have hundreds or thousands of cases, messages, or documents. This prevents slow page loads and improves overall performance.

Q: How do I see more items at once?

A: Use the Previous/Next buttons or page numbers to navigate through all your items. The system shows exactly which items you're viewing (e.g., "Showing 1-20 of 150").

Q: What happens when I search?

A: Searching automatically resets to page 1 and shows results across all your data, not just the current page. This ensures you find what you're looking for even if it would normally be on a different page.

üìÅ Case Management

Q: Why can't I see my closed cases?

A: The Cases page defaults to showing "Active Cases" only. Click "Closed" or "All" in the filter options to see your closed cases.

Q: How do I view detailed case information?

A: Click on any case in the list to open a large detailed view showing timeline, documents, messages, and payments all in one place.

Q: Can I search for cases that are closed?

A: Yes! The search function automatically searches across all cases, regardless of your current filter setting. You'll find both active and closed cases in search results.

üìÑ Documents & Files

Q: How are my documents organised?

A: Documents are grouped by case and displayed 20 per page. Each document shows the case it belongs to, upload date, file size, and type.

Q: What file types can I upload?

A: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, XLSX, XLS, CSV files up to 10MB each.

Q: Can I download documents?

A: Yes, click the download button next to any document. All downloads are secure and tracked for your organisation.
